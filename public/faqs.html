In VIT we have grades as S,A,B,C etc .We get it based on the standard derivative which is calculated of the whole class .Depending on how you have performed with respect to other students grades are awarded. S is 10gpa ,A is 9 ,B is 8 and so on.

FFCS is where you can choose your own teachers . A list of teachers come out and the subjects they would be taking in the respective semester .You can make your timetable based on you choice of teachers and make your timetable based on morning or afternoon slots. There is different time slots of the ffcs day provided of the students of yearwise in which they can register for the course.It is based on first come first book basis.

In VIT forget the old typical way of studying from thousands of books .Students usually study from the slides uploaded by the teacher and get the pdf of the book prescribed to them by the faculty

We have 3 question paper based exams per semester CAT 1 - it is of total 50 marks but is converted into 15.It is a closed book exam CAT 2 - it is of total 50 marks but is converted into 15.It is an open notebook exam.Students can take their notes prepared by them to the exam .Only handwritten notes are allowed in exam.No photocopies, no books are allowed. FAT - it is of total 100 marks but is converted into 40.It is a closed book exam.It is the final exam of each sem
